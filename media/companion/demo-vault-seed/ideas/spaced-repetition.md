# Spaced repetition

Review on an expanding schedule so recall effort lands just before forgetting. Cheap to
automate, brutal if the cards are vague.

Works best on top of [[ideas/evergreen-notes]] — you review *claims*, not highlights. It is
the engine behind the [[projects/reading-system]].
